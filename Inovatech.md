### B. Problem Overview
* **Manual Inventory Management**: Difficulty in tracking manual stock levels and ingredient usage lead to frequent inaccuracies.
* **Lack of Real-time Monitoring**: No immediate visibility into production costs, sales revenue, or daily wastage.
* **Inefficient Stock Alerts**: Manual checking of stock levels results in production delays due to unforeseen stockouts.

---

### C. System Solution
* **Comprehensive Web-Based Platform**: A centralized system to manage inventory, production, and purchase orders efficiently.
* **Automated Ingredient Tracking**: Features a FIFO-based deduction system that automatically updates inventory levels during production.
* **Data-Driven Insights**: Real-time dashboards provide visual analytics for inventory value, production trends, and profitability.

### E. Key Features
* ✔ **Universal Barcode System**: Integrated scanning for inventory, production, and physical counts.
* ✔ **Automated Ingredient Deduction**: Precise FIFO-based tracking of ingredients for every menu item produced.
* ✔ **Smart Low Stock Alerts**: Automated email notifications and visual triggers for critical stock levels.
* ✔ **Real-time Cost & Profit Analysis**: Instant calculation of unit costs, total sales, and profit margins.
* ✔ **Multi-item Purchase Orders**: Streamlined procurement with automated inventory replenishment.
